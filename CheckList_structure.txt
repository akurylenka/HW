What should be on the checklist?
List of checks (with required level of detail)
Check status:
build that was tested
test environment (if applicable)
tester
Result of checking

To create a working checklist, pay attention to these recommendations:
1. One point = one check
2. When compiling a checklist, you need to rely on requirements
3. Name checklist items in a form common to all team members
4. Detail the checklist depending on the task.
5. Combine checklists into matrices
